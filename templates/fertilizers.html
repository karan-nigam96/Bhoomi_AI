Soil Nutrition

Fertilizer Guide for High Yield

ICAR-recommended fertilizers and doses for maximum productivity. Based on NPK soil test recommendations.

🌾 Wheat
Urea + DAP + MOP
N 120 kg/ha P 60 kg/ha K 40 kg/ha

Split urea application: 50% basal + 50% at first irrigation (CRI stage). DAP as basal dose.

🌾 Rice
Urea + SSP + MOP
N 120 kg/ha P 60 kg/ha K 60 kg/ha

Apply N in 3 splits: transplanting, tillering & panicle initiation. Zinc sulphate 25 kg/ha for deficient soils.

🌽 Maize
Urea + DAP + Potash
N 120 kg/ha P 60 kg/ha K 40 kg/ha

Full P & K as basal. N in 3 splits: sowing, knee height, tasselling. Add Zn @ 25 kg ZnSO₄/ha.

🎋 Sugarcane
Urea + SSP + SOP
N 250 kg/ha P 115 kg/ha K 115 kg/ha

Apply N in 4 splits over 12 months. Trash mulching retains soil moisture and reduces weed pressure.

🌿 All Crops
Organic Amendments
FYM Compost Biofert

FYM @ 10–15 t/ha improves soil organic carbon. Rhizobium + PSB biofertilizers reduce chemical input by 20–25%.

💧 Micronutrients
Zinc · Boron · Iron
ZnSO₄ Borax FeSO₄

Zinc deficiency prevalent in Indo-Gangetic plains. Apply ZnSO₄ @ 25 kg/ha once every 3 years as preventive.
