About BhoomiAI

Random Forest — 10 Features, 300 Trees

BhoomiAI uses a scikit-learn Random Forest Classifier trained on ICAR-verified synthetic data covering pan-India crop conditions. The model achieves 92.4% test accuracy across 4 major Indian crops — with temperature auto-computed from zone, so farmers never need a weather station.

📊 Model Details

AlgorithmRandom Forest Classifier
Trees (n_estimators)300
Features10 (zone + season + soil)
ClassesRice, Wheat, Maize, Sugarcane
Test Accuracy92.4%
Frameworkscikit-learn (Python 3)
ScalerStandardScaler (pre-fit)
EncoderLabelEncoder → crop string

🔬 Input Features (10 total)

🤖 Auto-Computed (farmer never enters)

  • Zone_ID — 1–15 (GPS → ICAR zone mapping)
  • Season — 0 Kharif · 1 Rabi · 2 Zaid (device date)
  • Season_Temp — °C (NASA 21-yr zone×season table)
  • Temp_Range — °C (NASA 21-yr zone×season table)

✍ Farmer Enters

  • Nitrogen N — kg/ha (typical: 80–460)
  • Phosphorus P — kg/ha (typical: 6–48)
  • Potassium K — kg/ha (typical: 80–380)
  • Soil pH — 4.5 to 9.2
  • Soil Moisture — % (8–65%)
  • Irrigation — 1 (canal/tubewell) or 0 (rainfed)

🗺️ 15 ICAR Agro-Climatic Zones

GPS auto-detects zone · Manual dropdown fallback available · NASA 21-year climate averages used for Season_Temp & Temp_Range.

  • Zone 1 — Western Himalayan Region (HP, UK, J&K)
  • Zone 2 — Eastern Himalayan Region (Assam, NE)
  • Zone 3 — Lower Gangetic Plains (WB, S Bihar)
  • Zone 4 — Middle Gangetic Plains (E UP, N Bihar)
  • Zone 5 — Upper Gangetic Plains (W UP, Haryana)
  • Zone 6 — Trans-Gangetic Plains (Punjab, Delhi)
  • Zone 7 — Eastern Plateau & Hills (Jharkhand, Odisha)
  • Zone 8 — Central Plateau & Hills (MP, Bundelkhand)
  • Zone 9 — Western Plateau (Maharashtra, Marathwada)
  • Zone 10 — Southern Plateau (Karnataka, Telangana)
  • Zone 11 — East Coast (AP, Tamil Nadu coast)
  • Zone 12 — West Coast (Kerala, Goa)
  • Zone 13 — Gujarat Plains & Hills
  • Zone 14 — Western Dry Region (Rajasthan desert)
  • Zone 15 — Islands (Andaman & Nicobar)

📄 Model Pipeline

The model is stored as three files in newModel/: crop_model.pkl (Random Forest, 300 trees), scaler.pkl (StandardScaler), and label_encoder.pkl (LabelEncoder). The 10-feature vector is assembled server-side — temperature is looked up, never typed by the farmer.

Model trained on ICAR-verified synthetic data covering pan-India crop conditions. Accuracy: 92.4% on held-out test set.
